# Major AI Mindset Educational Curricula

## Foundations Course: "The 10 Pillars of the Major AI Mindset"

**Duration**: 4 weeks (8 sessions, 2 per week)
**Format**: Online or in-person, cohort-based
**Level**: Introductory

### Week 1: Foundation and Philosophy
#### Session 1: The Caribbean Digital Renaissance
- Historical context of technology in the Caribbean
- From extraction to empowerment
- The diaspora connection and opportunity
- Introduction to the 10 Pillars framework

#### Session 2: Mindset Fundamentals
- Breaking survival programming
- Building sovereign thinking patterns
- The spiritual foundations of wealth creation
- Assessment: Personal mindset mapping

### Week 2: Technical Foundations
#### Session 3: Technology Sovereignty Basics
- Introduction to blockchain and decentralization
- AI fundamentals for non-technical participants
- Data ownership and digital rights
- Exercise: Creating your technology sovereignty plan

#### Session 4: Community & Collaboration Infrastructure
- Building effective collective structures
- Decision-making frameworks for communities
- Resource pooling and distribution models
- Exercise: Mapping your ideal collaboration ecosystem

### Week 3: Implementation Fundamentals
#### Session 5: Personal Implementation
- Creating your pillar implementation roadmap
- Building daily practice systems
- Establishing accountability mechanisms
- Exercise: 30-60-90 day personal action plan

#### Session 6: Community Implementation
- Bringing the pillars to your existing networks
- Creating pillar-aligned initiatives
- Measuring community-level impact
- Exercise: Community opportunity mapping

### Week 4: Integration and Next Steps
#### Session 7: Case Studies and Models
- Review of successful implementations
- Lessons from failures and pivots
- Adapting models to your specific context
- Exercise: Model selection and adaptation

#### Session 8: Your Major Move
- Finalizing your implementation plan
- Connecting with resources and supporters
- Joining ongoing communities
- Final Assessment: Implementation readiness
